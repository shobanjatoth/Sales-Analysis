# Regional Sales Analysis (U.S. Market)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** and business intelligence solution analyzing 5 years of historical sales data across various United States regions. 

The primary business objective is to resolve inconsistent revenue and profit performance across regions, uncover hidden seasonal swings, isolate top-performing SKUs, evaluate sales channel margins, and build a strategic framework to optimize sales operations and regional marketing distribution.

---

## 📊 Business Problem & Approach
### The Problem
Sales leadership currently lacks automated, deep-dive visibility into regional performance. This gaps makes it difficult to proactively capture seasonal shifts, align supply chain stock with top SKUs, or distinguish high-margin channels from high-volume channels.

### The Solution: A Two-Phase Framework
1. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Deep dive into historical sales datasets using statistical aggregations to surface trends, structural anomalies, outliers, and margin drivers.
2. **Interactive Business Dashboard:** A live, multi-page 3D slicing environment built for business users to filter performance by time, product SKU, sales channel, and region.

---

## 📈 Key Insights & Discoveries

### 1. High-Performing Hubs vs. Optimization Targets
* **California** stands out as the anchor region, yielding elite revenue and margin consistency. 
* Structural performance variances are highly visible across the **Northeast** and **Midwest**, which require targeted, data-backed promotional investments.

### 2. Product Portfolio (SKU) Performance
* **Top Drivers:** Portfolio revenue is heavily anchor-weighted by top-performing items, specifically **Product 26** and **Product 25**.
* **Tail End:** Several low-margin SKUs introduce clear drag on overall profitability, indicating a need for a price adjustment or immediate product phase-out.

### 3. Channel Dynamics & Seasonality
* **Channels:** The **Export** channel delivers premium profitability, whereas high-volume **Wholesale** pipelines present a clear opportunity for structured volume bundling packages.
* **Seasonality swings:** High seasonal volatility is observed throughout the fiscal year—characterized by significant revenue surges in **January**, contrasted by severe transactional dips in **April**.

---

## 🛠️ Data-Driven Strategic Recommendations

* **🎯 Target Regional Investment:** Replicate California's high-performing market plays in other regions. Scale up targeted hyper-local marketing allocations throughout the Northeast and Midwest.
* **📦 Product SKU Optimization:** Double down inventory funding for Product 26 & 25. Systematically re-evaluate pricing tiers or completely phase out low-margin, bottom-tier SKUs.
* **⏳ Execute Seasonal Promotions:** Deploy tactical customer-recovery marketing campaigns in April to smooth out revenue drops, while aggressively amplifying introductory bundle offers in January.
* **📈 Channel Expansion Strategy:** Incentivize premium-margin Export partnerships, and structurally engineer volume deals to stabilize and lift margins in the Wholesale channel.
* **🛑 Margin Guardrails & Monitoring:** Flag all incoming enterprise orders yielding a profit margin under **80%** for immediate corporate cost-driver analysis.
